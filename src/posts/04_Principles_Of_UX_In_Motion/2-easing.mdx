<SlideTemplate title="Principle 1: Easing">

Disney: ‘Slow In and Slow Out.’  
Ux: continuity  
Both examples have the exact number of frames and take place over the exact same amount of time. The only difference is in their **easing**.  
Why ? Easings helps making your timing not too slow, not too fast. Always use easings.  

</SlideTemplate>
