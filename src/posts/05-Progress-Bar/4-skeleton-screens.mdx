<SlideTemplate title="What about Skeleton screens?">

![figma](/gif/skeleton_screens.gif)
_Figma for example, uses this technique_

Skeleton screens provide **incremental progress in loading the interface**. You can imagine them as blank pages (placeholders) with step-by-step loading of images, text and other content you might see on the page.

</SlideTemplate>

<Hint>
  This term firstly appeared by Luke Wroblewski, he advised using skeleton
  screens for a better loading experience. This idea was supported by other
  designers in the space and used in the UI of Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube,
  Google Drive & others. Skeleton screens provide immediate and spacial
  feedback.
</Hint>
